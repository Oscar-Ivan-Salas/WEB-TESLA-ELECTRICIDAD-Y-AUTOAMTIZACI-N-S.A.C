# Design Specifications

## Identity
- **Primary Color**: Dark Red / Garnet (#4A0404 or similar based on visual reference)
- **Secondary**: Black (#000000)
- **Accent**: Electric Gold / Yellow (#FFD700)
- **Typography**: Clean, sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Roboto) for high readability.

## Components
- **Hero**: Full screen, strong value proposition, "Talk to Technical Advisor" CTA.
- **Problem/Solution**: High contrast sections.
- **CTA**: Unique access point is the Chatbot.

## Responsiveness
- Mobile-first approach.
- Stacked layouts for smaller screens.
